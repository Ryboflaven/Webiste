Flames of Tradition

Flames of Tradition is a publication created from my dissertation writings on the decline of national bonfire night celebrations. Being from lewes I have been involved in their Bonfires for 20 years now. This publicaton is desinged and build around the idea of a bonfire, paper weights decline through reading the publication. Each reading page has a dedicated folding peramiter with the image pagers being printed through RISO. It is bound with real fire wokr fuse and has a flash paper colourphone, bring back the publication to the fireplace.
